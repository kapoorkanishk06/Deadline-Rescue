### **A guilt-free task prioritization app for overwhelmed minds**

## What is Deadline Rescue?

Deadline Rescue is a productivity app designed for moments when everything feels urgent, overwhelming, and mentally heavy. Instead of forcing users to plan perfectly, the app helps them make realistic decisions under limited time and energy.
It reframes productivity from “do everything” to “do what actually matters today.”

## The Problem:

1. Traditional to-do apps assume:
2. Unlimited time
3. Unlimited energy
4. Perfect discipline
5. In reality, users face:
6. Cognitive overload
7. Decision fatigue
8. Guilt from unfinished tasks
9. Paralysis when too many tasks compete for attention
This leads to avoidance, burnout, and eventually giving up on planning tools altogether.

## The Solution

Deadline Rescue introduces a constraint-first workflow:
 Users define:
   1. Available time (e.g. 4 hours)
   2. Current energy level (Low / Medium / High)
   3. Users dump all tasks without worrying about priority
   4. The app forces trade-offs
   5. Some tasks are intentionally dropped
   6. Users are reassured that dropping tasks is not failure, but strategy
   7. The result is a clear, achievable action list—without guilt.

## Key Features

 Context-aware prioritization based on time & energy
 Brain dump first, decisions later workflow
 Intentional task dropping to reduce guilt
 Minimal UI focused on clarity, not pressure
 Available as a web app (no install needed)

## Target Users

- Students facing academic overload
- Individuals with deadline anxiety
- People struggling with focus, burnout, or decision fatigue
- Anyone who wants progress over perfection

## Why It’s Different

- Deadline Rescue does not optimize productivity.
- It optimizes mental survival under pressure.
- Instead of asking “How do I do more?”
- It asks “What is realistically possible today?”
